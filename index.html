About Me

A creative practice shaped by observation, service, and care.

For more than a decade, I've built my career around creating meaningful experiences through hospitality, where I learned that the smallest details often leave the biggest impression. Later, as a Product Owner, I collaborated with cross-functional teams to bring ideas to life, strengthening my organization, communication, and problem-solving skills. While I enjoyed that work, I've always found myself drawn back to creating with my hands and expressing my creativity in more tangible ways.

Nature has always been where I find inspiration. Whether caring for houseplants, exploring the outdoors, or designing floral arrangements, I've always been captivated by the beauty, texture, and movement found in the natural world. My love of travel has introduced me to different floral traditions, plant varieties, and design styles, broadening the way I see flowers and the stories they can tell.

I believe flowers have a remarkable way of slowing us down. They celebrate life's happiest moments, offer comfort when words fall short, and bring beauty into everyday spaces. It's hard not to smile when you're looking at flowers, and I love being part of creating that feeling. I'm happiest with my sleeves rolled up, helping bring ideas to life with care, creativity, and intention through arrangements that are thoughtful, beautiful, unique, and memorable.
